Learning in a New Frontier

Online-Classroom-Learning

The online classroom is a new environment to the world where people can take classes from Primmary school to college. This is a new way of learning that has been around for a few years. This new medium has allowed people to learn from home with teachers and professors, in contrast to only being able to learn by oneself at home. This new medium has expanded peoples opportunity to learn and expand knowledge. However the difficulties that come with this new medium is uncharted territory for many. No matter one's race, age or any other factor, obstacles can appear for any individual. But, how does one overcome these obstacles? That is the question being tackled on this page.

    • Finding the Best Environment

    Having a safe space where one can study is greatly influential for the entirety of a persons studying. Knowing which environment is best for you is key to having a successful study session. Some people may prefer a quiet space, while others may prefer a more lively space. Some people may prefer to study in a group, while others may prefer to study alone. Knowing which environment is best for you is key to having a successful study session. Whether it be complete silence in a library or the light backfround noise of a coffee shop. The key is knowing oneself as an individual studyer.

    Collaborating With Classmates

    In order to truly be successful in the online learning environment, collaboration is essential. Without relying on others bumps in the road can become mountains.

    Time Management

    Time management is key to being successful in the online learning environment. Without proper time management, one can easily fall behind in their studies.

    Goal Setting

    Setting goals is important because it gives a person attainable goals with a clear path. Without this many just roam in indifference"
