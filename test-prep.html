Test Preparation

There are plenty of ways to prepare for a test that are applicable across disciplines, regardless what you are studying. Here are a few ideas to get you started, and acing that exam!

  • Study in a group
  • Use flashcards
  • Take practice tests
  • Stay calm
  • Get a good night's sleep
