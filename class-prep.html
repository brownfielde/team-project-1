Preparing for Class:

Finding the best Environment

Finding an environment conducive to your success is paramount to acclimating to the online classroom setting.

Having a safe space where one can study is greatly influential for the entirety of a persons studying. Knowing which environment is best for you is key to having a successful study session. Some people may prefer a quiet space, while others may prefer a more lively space. Some people may prefer to study in a group, while others may prefer to study alone. Knowing which environment is best for you is key to having a successful study session. Whether it be complete silence in a library or the light backfround noise of a coffee shop. The key is knowing oneself as an individual studyer.
